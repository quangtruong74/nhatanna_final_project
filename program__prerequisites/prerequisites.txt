Applicants are not required to have a degree in computer science for entry to a program within the Department of Computer Science. 
Upon review of your application, the department will determine if the completion of prerequisite coursework will be required, based on your academic and professional background. 
The following prerequisite courses may be required:
METCS300 Introduction to Software Development
A maximum of two graduate-level courses (8 credits) taken at Metropolitan College before acceptance into the program may be applied towards the degree.
